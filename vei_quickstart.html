Why Predictive can be “Emerging”

Predictive Strength answers: “How strong is the relationship between constructs and an outcome?”
Reliability gating answers: “Is the evidence stable enough to operationalize?”

Even if a relationship looks strong, the system stays conservative if reliability and sample sufficiency aren’t at decision-grade thresholds yet.

Why Actions still shows “what to do”

Actions is a decision co-pilot: it recommends safe next steps even under uncertainty (e.g., tighten clarity, adjust reward framing, run a narrow A/B test, or collect more Tier-3).

The mismatch is intentional: responsible analytics + governance, not “dashboard theater.”

What “ORG” means in this demo

ORGs are example environments used to demonstrate different maturity states (volume, reliability, and response patterns). They’re not meant to mirror actual HubSpot/Salesforce performance — they exist to show how outputs change as data becomes decision-grade.
